Training Programs

Beginner Full-Body Routine (3 Days/Week)

Focus: Strength, building a habit, learning form.


Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday (Rest in between).


Routine: Perform 3 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, resting 1-2 minutes between sets.


Squats
Chest Press
Row
Overhead Press
Lat Pulldowns
Core Workout

Intermediate PPL Split (4-6 Days/Week)

Focus: Hypertrophy (Muscle Growth), high volume.


Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps):
Bench press, Dumbbell military press, Lateral raises, Tricep extensions.


Pull (Back, Biceps):
Deadlifts, Pull-ups/Lat pulldowns, Dumbbell rows, Bicep curls.


Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes):
Squats, Lunges, Leg press, Romanian deadlift.

The 3-2-1 Method (6 Days/Week)

Focus: Balanced fitness (Strength + Cardio + Flexibility).


3 Strength Sessions: Focus on lifting weights.
2 Pilates/Mobility Sessions: Focus on core and flexibility.
1 Cardio Session: Focus on cardiovascular health.
1 Rest Day: Pahinga mo 'to, Boi

Visual Meal Plan

Muscle Building/Bulking

Focuses on a caloric surplus and high protein (259-302g) to build muscle mass.


BREAKFAST: Scrambled eggs, oats, and stir-fried veggies.
LUNCH: Grilled chicken, sweet potato, and mixed greens.
SNACK: Protein shake with banana.
DINNER: Salmon with brown rice and broccoli.

Cutting/Fat Loss

Focuses on a caloric deficit (approx. 2,550 kcal) to lose fat while keeping protein high (191-223g) to retain muscle.


BREAKFAST: Greek yogurt with berries and almonds.
LUNCH: Turkey breast with green beans.
SNACK: Hard-boiled eggs and carrot sticks.
DINNER: Grilled tilapia with zucchini and quinoa.

Easy Meal Prep

Designed for convenience, often using versatile staples like chicken, rice, and healthy fats.


MEAL 1: Egg whites, bacon, and rice cakes.
MEAL 2: Grilled chicken thigh, cilantro lime rice, pico de gallo, and guacamole.
MEAL 3: Steak with broccoli and roasted potatoes.
MEAL 4: Greek yogurt with protein powder and fruit.

High Protein/Strength

Prioritizes high-quality protein and carbs for energy and recovery.


BREAKFAST: Protein smoothie bowl with granola.
LUNCH: Tuna or chicken salad with whole-wheat wrap.
SNACK: Cottage cheese with pineapple.
DINNER: Lean lamb chop or chicken breast with sweet potato mash.
